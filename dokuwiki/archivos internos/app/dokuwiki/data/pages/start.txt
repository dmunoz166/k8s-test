====== About us ======

3HTP is a professional services company with presence in Chile, Colombia, Peru and the USA. It is exclusively oriented to cover all organizational and business technology needs, in the areas of Infrastructure Management (Middleware), Cloud Technology Development (Cloud) and Software Lifecycle Automation and Deployment Processes (DevOps).

3HTP is exclusively oriented to cover all the needs of companies and organizations in the disciplines of JEE, SOA and BPM.

To cover all the needs that derive from the management and use of this type of disciplines, the 3HTP approach is based on:

  * JEE, SOA / BPM Consulting and Architecture Services

  * JEE, SOA / BPM Implementation and development services 

  * Functional and performance testing services for distributed systems (JEE, SOA)

  * JEE, SOA / BPM Training and Coaching Services

  * Support and Operational Continuity Services for JEE, SOA / BPM Platforms

  * Monitoring Services
